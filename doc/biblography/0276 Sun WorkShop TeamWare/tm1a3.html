Navigate Menu

The Navigate Menu in the Merging Window provides the following commands:
Next Moves you down the list of difference. You have a choice of:
Difference Moves to the next difference in the current pane.
Resolved Difference Moves to the next resolved difference.
Unresolved Difference Moves to the next unresolved difference.
Left Difference Moves to the next difference in the left pane.
Right Difference Moves to the next difference in the right pane.
Previous Moves you down the list of difference. You have a choice of:
Difference Moves to the next difference in the current pane.
Resolved Difference Moves to the next resolved difference.
Unresolved Difference Moves to the next unresolved difference.
Left Difference Moves to the next difference in the left pane.
Right Difference Moves to the next difference in the right pane.
Selected Difference Moves to the select difference.
Find Opens the Find Dialog Box.
Go To Line Opens the Goto Line Dialog Box.
