Using the Online Help

The Sun WorkShopTM 6 and the Sun WorkShopTM TeamWare online help pages are divided into two panes:

  • The left pane lists online help topics for each section of the online help.
  • The right pane provides the content of the current help topic.

The online help is divided into sections (Working With Projects, Browsing Source Code, Using the Debugging Window, Using dbx Commands, and so on). Most sections have the following subsections:
How-Tos Provides step-by-step task-oriented instructions.
Concepts Describes conceptual information about Sun WorkShop or Sun WorkShop TeamWare topics.
Windows Describes dialog boxes and windows for this section of the online help.
Commands (Only available in the Using dbx Commands section of the online help) Provides dbx command syntax.

Click on the icon of the subsection you want.

Navigating in the Online Help

To view the online help contents or index:

  • Click on the Contents or Index icon in the upper left pane.

From the Contents page, to show the list of topics for an online help section:

  • Click on an online help section in the left pane.

To view the list of task-oriented instructions available for the current online help section:

  • Click the How-Tos icon or the How-Tos link underneath the icon.

To view the list of concepts for the current online help section:

  • Click the Concepts icon or the Concepts link underneath the icon.

To view a previously visited help topic in the right pane:

  • In the upper left pane, click the Back arrow to go back one topic. Click the Forward arrow to go forward one topic. (Using your browser's Back and Forward buttons will navigate sequentially through all of your previously visited sites, not just the right topic pane.)
