Understanding Access Control

You can grant or deny permission for individual users or groups to perform the following Sun WorkShopTM TeamWare transactions:

  • bringover-from

  • bringover-to

  • putback-from

  • putback-to

  • undo

  • workspace delete

  • workspace move

  • workspace reparent

  • workspace reparent-to

You can grant or deny permissions on an individual, group, (see Setting Individual or Group Permissions) or global basis (see Setting Global Permissions).

Note - Transactions can take longer when you set access control. If you grant or deny permissions to a large group, Sun WorkShop TeamWare configuring may take several seconds to look up the members of the group before executing a transaction.
