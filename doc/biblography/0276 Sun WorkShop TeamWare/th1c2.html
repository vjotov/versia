To sort transaction information in the Workspace History Viewer:

1. In the Configuring Window, click on a workspace to select it.

2. Choose Workspace View History.

3. In the Workspace History Viewer Window, choose View Options.

4. Click on one of the buttons in the Sort By section to select a criterion to sort by (Time, Operation, User, Host, TW Release, or Exit Status).

5. Click OK.
