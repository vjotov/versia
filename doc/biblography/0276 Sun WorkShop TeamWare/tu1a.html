Understanding Sun WorkShop TeamWare

Related Topics:

Hints from Other Users

Sun WorkShopTM TeamWare management tools are based on a concurrent development model called Copy-Modify-Merge. You copy files from a central workspace into your own workspace, modify the files, and then merge your changes into the central workspace.

Sun WorkShopTeamWare helps you manage your files using the following tools:
Configuring For setting up and managing workspaces (see Configuring Defined)
Versioning For checking files in and out, and viewing a file's history (see Versioning Defined)
Merging For merging the changes between your local workspace and a parent workspace (see Merging Defined)
Freezepointing For saving a snapshot of a workspace (see Freezepointing Defined)
